Moving to a free position

Consider the board on the right, where your bot is next to a soon-to-explode bomb. But now we can't use the concrete positions... each time you refresh this page the bot and bomb will be placed in different positions on the board.

How can the bot always escape the bomb?

  • We have four directions that we can consider: above, below, leftwards and rightwards.
  • It is not as simple as choosing one of them randomly, because we cannot move into a wall and the bot will die if it moves into the bomb.
  • We can define a isFreePos function which receives a position and tests precisely if it is free (not a wall nor a bomb). This function is also available in the bot interface.
  • Our bot then moves to the first free position: up, down, left or right in order.
