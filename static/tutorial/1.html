Moving in a direction

Consider the board on the right, with both your bot and a power-up in a fixed position.

How can the bot pick up the power-up?

  • The bot will have to make a series of moves to the right, in the direction of the power-up.
  • We just instruct the bot to move to the right.
  • Note that the bot function repeats itself over time, each time it will move a step to the right.
