Moving in multiple directions

Consider the board on the right, with both your bot and a power-up in a fixed position.

How can the bot pick up the power-up?

  • We now have to give different move instructions to the bot... the simplest path is to move to the right, and then move down.
  • But how can the bot decide on where to move? For that we need to use the state, which us gives up-to-date information about the board and the players. In this example, we want to know the position of the bot.
  • Then we want to choose the bot's move depending on its position. In programming, this is done using an if-then-else statement. In this example, we can move to the right until the bot's column is smaller than 7, and keep moving down afterwards.
